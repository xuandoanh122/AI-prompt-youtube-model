## 1. Director's decision

- **Cau hoi:** `Which sound is soft?`
- **Dap an:** silver wind chime.
- **Fact:** `Some sounds are loud. Some sounds are soft.`
- **Ba lua chon, trai -> phai:** red toy drum · silver wind chime · yellow whistle.
- **Visual proof:** drum phat vong song am thanh lon mau cam; wind chime phat vong song nho mau xanh nhat; whistle dung yen trong reveal de tranh qua tai. Dap an soft duoc nhin thay bang song nho.
- **Y do:** video nay cho phep SFX la nhan vat chinh; mix drum to that, chime nho that, nhung khong de nhac che voice.
- **Primary fidelity spend:** sound-wave size + SFX loudness contrast. Nouri identity la secondary spend.

## 2. Episode locks

**SET BLOCK:**

```text
Setting: a bright children's music room with a soft round pastel rug, low white shelves with a few simple instruments blurred in the background, warm morning light from a window, and clean upper wall space for large readable words.
```

**SOUND OPTIONS BLOCK:**

```text
On the rug, from left to right: one small red toy drum with two short wooden drumsticks resting beside it, one delicate silver wind chime hanging from a low simple stand, and one small yellow whistle. The three sound objects are child-safe, clearly separated, similarly sized on screen, and remain in those exact positions.
```

**SOUND WAVE LOGIC LOCK:**

```text
Sound volume is shown as simple animated rings: loud sounds make large warm orange rings, soft sounds make small pale blue rings. Only the object being demonstrated creates sound rings. No object changes position unless the Action section says so.
```

**PRE-REVEAL FIREWALL:**

```text
In Hook and Look, no sound rings, vibration, glow, arrow, check mark, character reaction or sound cue may identify the soft answer. All three objects receive equal framing, light and attention until Reveal.
```

## 3. Beat map

| Clip | Job va hinh anh | Exact text + thoai | Audio/endpoint |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 · Hook · 6s | Nouri sau ba object; ear bounce + paw sweep roi dung. | `WHICH SOUND IS SOFT?`; Nouri: `Which sound is soft?` | Motif mo dau, no instrument hit. |
| 2 · Look + Guess · 8s | Camera glide drum -> chime -> whistle; tat ca im lang. Countdown. | VO: `Look carefully... which one sounds soft?`; `3` `2` `1` | Marimba notes equal, tick countdown. |
| 3 · Reveal · 8s | Drum tao vong cam lon 1 lan; chime tao vong xanh nho 1 lan; Nouri chi chime roi noi. | `THE CHIME!` -> `SOFT SOUND`; Nouri: `The wind chime makes a soft sound!` | Drum hit loud but short; chime soft; reveal chime. |
| 4 · Fact · 8s | Split teaching view: big orange rings labeled loud, small blue rings labeled soft. | `LOUD` -> `SOFT`; VO: `Some sounds are loud. Some sounds are soft.` | Loud low drum hit, soft chime tinkle, duck VO. |
| 5 · CTA · 8s | Nouri hoi 3s, im 2s, thumbs-up. | `WHISPER SOFTLY`; Nouri: `Can you whisper your name very softly?` | Music off 3-5s; motif + pop. |
