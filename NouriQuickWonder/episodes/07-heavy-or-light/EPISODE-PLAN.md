## 1. Director's decision

- **Cau hoi:** `Which one is easy to lift?`
- **Dap an:** red balloon.
- **Fact:** `Light things are easy to lift. Heavy things are hard to lift.`
- **Ba lua chon, trai -> phai:** red balloon tied to a small weight · thick blue book · purple bowling ball.
- **Visual proof:** Reveal chi cho Nouri giu balloon string; balloon bay len nhe va keo paw Nouri len mot cach vui ve. Book va bowling ball khong di chuyen trong reveal.
- **Primary fidelity spend:** balloon lift motion + Nouri reaction. Comedy vat ly ro nhung khong qua tai.

## 2. Episode locks

**SET BLOCK:**

```text
Setting: a sunny grassy playground with a simple low wooden bench, a soft blue sky, a few blurred playground shapes far behind, and warm late-afternoon golden light. The frame has clean upper space for large readable words.
```

**WEIGHT OPTIONS BLOCK:**

```text
On the bench, from left to right: one bright red helium balloon tied by a thin string to a tiny round weight, one thick blue hardcover book, and one glossy purple bowling ball with three finger holes. The three objects are child-safe, clearly separated, similarly sized on screen, and remain in those exact positions.
```

**WEIGHT LOGIC LOCK:**

```text
Lightness is shown by upward motion and ease. Heaviness is shown by staying low and still. Only the red balloon moves in the Reveal. The book and bowling ball remain fully still until the Fact clip.
```

## 3. Beat map

| Clip | Job va hinh anh | Exact text + thoai | Audio/endpoint |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 · Hook · 6s | Nouri sau bench, paw sweep qua 3 vat. | `EASY TO LIFT?`; Nouri: `Which one is easy to lift?` | Motif, soft swish. |
| 2 · Look + Guess · 8s | Glide balloon -> book -> bowling ball; countdown. | VO: `Look carefully... which one looks light?`; `3` `2` `1` | Equal marimba notes. |
| 3 · Reveal · 8s | Balloon bay len keo paw Nouri nhe; Nouri cuoi va noi. | `THE BALLOON!` -> `LIGHT!`; Nouri: `The balloon is easy to lift!` | Airy whoosh + reveal chime. |
| 4 · Fact · 8s | Teaching scale: feather/balloon side rises, bowling ball side stays down. | `LIGHT` -> `HEAVY`; VO: `Light things are easy to lift. Heavy things are hard to lift.` | Upward glock, low thump. |
| 5 · CTA · 8s | Nouri hoi 3s, im 2s, thumbs-up. | `YOUR TURN!`; Nouri: `Can you lift something light?` | Silence 3-5s, motif + pop. |
