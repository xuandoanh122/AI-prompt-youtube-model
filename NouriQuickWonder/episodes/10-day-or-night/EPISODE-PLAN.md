## 1. Director's decision

- **Cau hoi:** `Which sky shows night?`
- **Dap an:** middle night sky card.
- **Fact:** `We often see the moon and stars at night.`
- **Ba lua chon, trai -> phai:** sunny day sky · moon-and-stars night sky · sunset sky.
- **Visual proof:** Reveal lam middle card sang len nhe; moon va stars twinkle mot lan; day va sunset cards fade nhe sau khi dap an da ro.
- **Primary fidelity spend:** sky card shapes + middle-card twinkle. Khong bien full background thanh dem de tranh style jump.

## 2. Episode locks

**SET BLOCK:**

```text
Setting: a cozy window-side learning nook with a low pale wooden display table, a soft cream wall, a large round window showing gentle daylight outside, and clean upper wall space for large readable words.
```

**SKY CARD OPTIONS BLOCK:**

```text
On the display table, from left to right: one rectangular sky card showing a bright yellow sun in a blue daytime sky, one rectangular sky card showing a silver crescent moon and three small white stars in a deep blue night sky, and one rectangular sky card showing an orange sunset sky with a low sun near the horizon. The three cards are upright, similarly sized, clearly separated, and remain in those exact positions.
```

**SKY LOGIC LOCK:**

```text
Night is represented only by the deep blue sky card with a crescent moon and stars. The day card and sunset card remain still and do not gain stars. Only the night card twinkles during Reveal, and only after the reveal begins.
```
