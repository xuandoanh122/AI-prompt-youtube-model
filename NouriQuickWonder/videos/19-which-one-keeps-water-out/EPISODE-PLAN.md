## 1. Director's decision

- **Câu hỏi:** Which one is made to keep water out?
- **Đáp án:** the yellow rain boot.
- **Fact:** Waterproof materials help stop water passing through.
- **Ba lựa chọn, trái → phải:** yellow rain boot material swatch · brown paper swatch · blue cotton swatch.
- **Visual proof:** Ba dropper thả ba giọt bằng nhau; giọt trên yellow swatch bead rồi lăn xuống tray, paper sẫm, cotton hút.
- **Production model:** modular independent clips; không extend, không previous-video reference.
- **Primary fidelity spend:** physical proof và endpoint; Nouri identity là secondary spend.

## 2. Episode locks

**SET BLOCK**

~~~text
Setting: a bright rain-gear test corner with a pale wooden bench, one wide white catch tray, three fixed clear droppers above it, a mint wall with a blurred coat hook, soft window light, and clean upper space for large readable words.
~~~

**OPTIONS BLOCK**

~~~text
On three identical low frames, from left to right: one smooth yellow rain-boot material swatch, one plain brown paper swatch, and one soft blue cotton swatch. Each swatch sits above the white catch tray beneath one identical fixed clear dropper.
~~~

**LOGIC LOCK**

~~~text
Three equal water drops test the surfaces at the same moment. The drop on the yellow waterproof swatch beads and rolls off into the tray; the paper darkens and the cotton absorbs its drop. Only the yellow swatch keeps water from passing into it.
~~~

## 4. Điểm nghẽn và quyết định

- Pre-reveal: Clip 1 và Clip 2 trước khi countdown kết thúc giữ ba lựa chọn cùng framing, light và attention; không cue bằng motion, glow hoặc sound.
- P2-B chỉ đổi reveal timing: physical proof bắt đầu khoảng giây tổng 9.7 và hoàn tất khoảng 11.8 trong Clip 2.
- Clip 3 restage completed evidence để trả lời; không replay droppers hoặc tạo proof lần hai.
- Sai đáp án: The paper and cotton stay fixed in their frames; paper darkening and cotton absorption do not reverse or spread beyond one small patch.
- Nouri dừng body/head/paw trước khi nói; camera khóa trong thoại.
- Exact text sai một ký tự, endpoint sai hoặc effect giống phép thuật đều phải retake.
