## Episode lock

- **Question:** Which one will roll down the ramp?
- **Answer:** the red rubber ball.
- **Fact:** Objects with curved sides can roll.
- **Options:** red rubber ball · blue wooden cube · closed yellow picture book.
- **Proof mechanism:** only the ball-lane stopper lowers; gravity moves the ball down the ramp into a padded tray.
- **No-magic lock:** every motion comes from Nouri's visible paw, a visible mechanical stopper or gravity.
