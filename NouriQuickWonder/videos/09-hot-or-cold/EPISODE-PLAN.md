## 1. Director's decision

- **Cau hoi:** `Which one is cold?`
- **Dap an:** ice cube.
- **Fact:** `Cold things can make tiny water drops outside.`
- **Ba lua chon, trai -> phai:** warm red mug · clear ice cube · soft blue mitten.
- **Visual proof:** ice cube tao hoi lanh xanh nhe va giot nuoc ngung tu tren dia nho; mug va mitten dung yen, khong scary heat.
- **Primary fidelity spend:** condensation droplets + cold mist around ice cube.

## 2. Episode locks

**SET BLOCK:**

```text
Setting: a bright safe picnic table under soft morning shade, with a pale wooden tabletop, a blurred green park background, gentle sunlight, and clean upper space for large readable words.
```

**TEMPERATURE OPTIONS BLOCK:**

```text
On the table, from left to right: one warm red mug with a lid and a tiny safe curl of steam, one clear ice cube sitting on a small white saucer, and one soft blue winter mitten. The three objects are child-safe, clearly separated, similarly sized on screen, and remain in those exact positions.
```

**TEMPERATURE LOGIC LOCK:**

```text
Cold is shown only by pale blue cool mist and tiny water droplets around the ice cube. The mug stays closed and safe with only a tiny gentle steam curl. No hand touches any object, no fire appears, and no burn or pain is shown.
```

## 3. Beat map

| Clip | Job va hinh anh | Exact text + thoai | Audio/endpoint |

| 1 · Hook · 6s | Nouri sau table, paw sweep. | `WHICH ONE IS COLD?`; Nouri: `Which one is cold?` | Motif, no temperature cue. |

| 5 · CTA · 8s | Nouri hoi 3s, im 2s, thumbs-up. | `FIND COLD`; Nouri: `Can you find something cold?` | Silence 3-5s, motif + pop. |
