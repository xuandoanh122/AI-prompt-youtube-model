## 1. Director's decision

- **Câu hỏi:** Which two paint colors make green?
- **Đáp án:** blue and yellow paint.
- **Fact:** Blue and yellow paint mix to make green.
- **Ba lựa chọn, trái → phải:** blue + yellow · red + blue · red + yellow.
- **Visual proof:** Paddle nhìn thấy được trong blue+yellow chamber quay đúng một vòng và tạo green paint; hai chamber khác giữ màu tách riêng.
- **Production model:** modular independent clips; không extend, không previous-video reference.
- **Primary fidelity spend:** physical proof và endpoint; Nouri identity là secondary spend.

## 2. Episode locks

Setting: a bright art-science color station with a wide pale-wood table, three sealed clear mixing chambers with visible central paddles, soft aqua walls, cream shelves out of focus, and clean upper space for large readable words.
~~~

**OPTIONS BLOCK**

~~~text
From left to right: one sealed chamber holding separate blue and yellow paint pools, one holding separate red and blue paint pools, and one holding separate red and yellow paint pools. Each chamber has one visible white mixing paddle and remains sealed.
~~~

**LOGIC LOCK**

~~~text
Only the blue-and-yellow chamber is tested during Reveal. Its visible paddle turns one slow rotation, combining those two paint pools into one clean green pool. The other two paddles and color pairs remain separate and still.
~~~

## 4. Điểm nghẽn và quyết định

- Clips 1–2 giữ equal framing/light/attention và không cue đáp án.
- Reveal chỉ có một physical test với thiết bị hoặc source nhìn thấy được.
- Sai đáp án: The red-blue and red-yellow chambers remain unmixed with paddles stopped and colors clearly separate.
- Nouri dừng body/head/paw trước thoại; camera khóa khi nói.
- Sai text, endpoint hoặc effect giống phép thuật đều phải retake.
