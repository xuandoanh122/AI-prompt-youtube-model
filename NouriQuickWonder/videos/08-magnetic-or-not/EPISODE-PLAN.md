## 1. Director's decision

- **Cau hoi:** `Which one sticks to the magnet?`
- **Dap an:** silver paperclip.
- **Fact:** `Magnets pull some metal things.`
- **Ba lua chon, trai -> phai:** wooden block · silver paperclip · plastic button.
- **Visual proof:** red horseshoe magnet tien gan tu tren xuong; chi paperclip truot len va dinh vao magnet. Block va button dung yen.
- **Primary fidelity spend:** magnet endpoint + paperclip attachment. Khong dung coin de tranh model lam ca coin bi hut.

## 2. Episode locks

**SET BLOCK:**

```text
Setting: a bright classroom science table with a clean pale wood surface, soft mint wall behind it, a few blurred child-safe science supplies on a high shelf, warm morning light, and clean upper space for large readable words.
```

**MAGNET OPTIONS BLOCK:**

```text
On the table, from left to right: one small natural wooden cube block, one shiny silver paperclip, and one round blue plastic button with two holes. A red horseshoe magnet is visible above the table only during Reveal and Fact clips. The three objects are child-safe, clearly separated, similarly sized on screen, and remain in those exact positions until the paperclip moves in Reveal.
```

**MAGNET LOGIC LOCK:**

```text
Only the silver paperclip is pulled by the red horseshoe magnet. The wooden block and plastic button never move, slide, shake, glow or attach. The magnet never touches the wooden block or plastic button.
```
