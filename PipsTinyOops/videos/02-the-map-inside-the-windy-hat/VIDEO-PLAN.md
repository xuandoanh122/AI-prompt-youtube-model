## Story lock

On a windy seaside pier, Pip tries to return a pale-yellow straw hat before it blows into the water. The hat repeatedly escapes along a strange route. When its seafoam lining flips outward, Pip discovers a stitched map whose three symbols match landmarks already passed. Pip follows the final shell symbol to a hidden sheltered garden and places the hat on its matching wooden garden peg, where it shades a tiny seedling.

## Dramatic spine

- **Pip wants:** catch and safely return the loose hat.
- **Obstacle:** gusts, pier geography and one hanging net keep the direct route from working.
- **Midpoint reinterpretation:** the hat is not moving randomly; its lining contains a landmark map.
- **Climax choice:** stop chasing directly and use the map route through the shell gate.
- **Resolution:** the hat belongs at the hidden garden peg and becomes shade for a seedling.
- **Comic button:** one final gust lifts only the brim; the blue ribbon catches gently on the peg and Pip gives a dry double blink.
