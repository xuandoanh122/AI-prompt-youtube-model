## Story lock

Pip must fit a tall sandwich made from soft felt play-food into a square lunchbox. Two harmless attempts fail for different visible reasons. Pip then rebuilds the same pieces as two short sandwiches side-by-side, and the lid closes easily.

- Same lunchbox, piece colors/count and table orientation across components.
- Pip never falls, cries, throws pieces or forces the hinge.
