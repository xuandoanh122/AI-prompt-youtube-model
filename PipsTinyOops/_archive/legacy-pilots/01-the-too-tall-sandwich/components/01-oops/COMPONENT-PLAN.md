# COMPONENT 01 — OOPS

> **Copy-ready shot guide:** [PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md](PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md) — full all-in-one prompts, take checklists and one-variable retakes.

> Job: make the problem readable in the first two seconds.
> Set: clean seaside-kitchen table; open square lunchbox; one tall felt sandwich; Pip at frame right.

| Shot | Action | Endpoint |
|---|---|---|
| 01 · 8s | Pip proudly presents the tall felt stack | Pip and stack hold in equal scale |
| 02 · 8s | Pip lowers the hinged lid once | lid touches top layer and stops visibly open |
| 03 · 8s | Pip releases the lid | lid rises back; one dry woodblock cue |
| 04 · 8s | Pip looks lid → stack → camera | one silent puzzled head tilt holds |

**Hard fail:** real food, crushed pieces, hidden cause, damaged hinge, extra character.
