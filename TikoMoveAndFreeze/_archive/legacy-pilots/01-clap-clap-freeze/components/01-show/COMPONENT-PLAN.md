# COMPONENT 01 — SHOW THE GAME

> **Copy-ready shot guide:** [PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md](PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md) — full all-in-one prompts, take checklists and one-variable retakes.

> Job: teach the two cues and complete one readable demo.
> Set: simple turquoise/coral play stage; full-body locked camera; cue card area above Tiko.

| Shot | Action | Endpoint |
|---|---|---|
| 01 · 8s | Tiko faces camera and gives one welcoming wave | full-body neutral ready pose |
| 02 · 8s | green `MOVE` cue appears; Tiko claps exactly twice | hands separate and stop |
| 03 · 8s | amber `FREEZE` cue appears with one woodblock hit | open-arm freeze holds one second |
| 04 · 8s | Tiko relaxes and says “Clap, clap, freeze!” | ready pose holds; cue clears |

**Hard fail:** cropped feet/hands, three claps, moving during FREEZE, extra dancer.
