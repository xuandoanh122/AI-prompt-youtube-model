# COMPONENT 04 — SURPRISE FREEZE

> **Copy-ready shot guide:** [PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md](PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md) — full all-in-one prompts, take checklists and one-variable retakes.

> Job: one fair timing surprise after the rule is understood.
> Start: Tiko and floor dots in ready state; cue shapes/colors unchanged.

| Shot | Action | Endpoint |
|---|---|---|
| 01 · 8s | green cue; Tiko performs side step and first clap | first clap completes |
| 02 · 8s | amber cue arrives before second clap | Tiko freezes immediately and safely |
| 03 · 8s | Tiko holds, then makes one playful eye glance toward missing clap | body stays frozen |
| 04 · 8s | green cue returns; Tiko completes two normal claps | rule resets clearly |

**Hard fail:** misleading cue color, unsafe stop mid-step, repeated surprise, frantic tempo.
