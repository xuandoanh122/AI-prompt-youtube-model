# COMPONENT 03 — BUILD THE PATTERN

> **Copy-ready shot guide:** [PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md](PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md) — full all-in-one prompts, take checklists and one-variable retakes.

> Job: add one safe side step without changing the core game.
> Start: stage adds two floor dots, one left and one center.

| Shot | Action | Endpoint |
|---|---|---|
| 01 · 8s | Tiko steps from center dot to left dot | both feet settle fully |
| 02 · 8s | Tiko claps exactly twice | hands separate and stop |
| 03 · 8s | amber cue appears | Tiko freezes with feet planted |
| 04 · 8s | Tiko repeats the whole pattern slowly once | final freeze holds |

**Hard fail:** jump/spin, sliding feet, changing dot positions, additional clap.
