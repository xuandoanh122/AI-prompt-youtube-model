# COMPONENT 02 — SLOW COPY

> **Copy-ready shot guide:** [PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md](PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md) — full all-in-one prompts, take checklists and one-variable retakes.

> Job: give the child honest response time.
> Start: Tiko in neutral ready pose; camera and stage unchanged.

| Shot | Action | Endpoint |
|---|---|---|
| 01 · 8s | Tiko demonstrates two slow claps | hands stop apart |
| 02 · 8s | Tiko says “Your turn” then remains still for four seconds | no music cue interrupts response gap |
| 03 · 8s | amber cue appears; Tiko enters open-arm freeze | pose holds one full second |
| 04 · 8s | Tiko gives one thumbs-up | neutral stance returns and holds |

**Hard fail:** fake response pause, background movement, cue before child gap completes.
