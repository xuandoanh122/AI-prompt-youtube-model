# COMPONENT 05 — FINAL ROUND

> **Copy-ready shot guide:** [PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md](PRODUCTION-RUNBOOK.md) — full all-in-one prompts, take checklists and one-variable retakes.

> Job: let the child perform from cues with minimal demo.
> Start: Tiko neutral; stage clear; cue area visible.

| Shot | Action | Endpoint |
|---|---|---|
| 01 · 8s | Tiko says “Ready?” and becomes completely still | three-second response gap completes |
| 02 · 8s | green cue pulses twice while Tiko only marks rhythm with eyes | child owns the clap action |
| 03 · 8s | amber cue and one woodblock hit | Tiko and background hold still |
| 04 · 8s | Tiko smiles, gives one wave and says “Great moving!” | final full-body pose holds for loop |

**Hard fail:** Tiko performs instead of child in Shot 02, no true freeze, extra speech/text.
